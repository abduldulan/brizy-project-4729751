Hendra Setiawan Boen

Hendra Setiawan Boen is a co-founder, principal, Managing Partner, and the Head of the Dispute Resolution Practice Group and Intellectual Property Practice Group of the firm. He reads law at the Faculty of Law of the University of Trisakti. Hendra has been practicing law since 2005, and registered as a Receiver and Administrator for Bankruptcy with the Ikatan Kurator dan Pengurus Indonesia (IKAPI). Hendra served as a legal team’s coordinator on behalf of Mr. Joko Widodo during his 2019 re-election campaign.

Hendra was a partner in a well-known and reputable litigation law firm, and previously worked for a first-tier corporate firm considered as one of the pioneers in the field of capital market law in Indonesia, before moving on to practice with an Indonesian first-tier litigation firm. There he handled numerous multifaceted complex litigations, be it corporate litigation or intellectual property litigation. He also actively contributes his time to write law articles, published in national-scale media, as well as a book on law.

Hendra has extensive experience in a wide variety of commercial-litigation-related work, ranging from company issues, marine insurance, charter party disputes, banking matters, property matters, financial and investment related claims, capital market issues, construction disputes, bankruptcy proceedings, anti-monopoly disputes, energy and resources matters, telecom matters, maritime issues, plantation issues to infrastructure sectors. He also specializes in intellectual property protection and advises in numerous class-action suits and citizen lawsuits as well as cross-border international arbitration

Hendra's work has been covered widely by local and international media, inter alia, as follows: Bisnis Indonesia, Katadata, Kontan, Tempo, Beritasatu, Hukumonline, Sindonews, Detik, Media Indonesia, MetroTV, CNN Indonesia, VOA Indonesia, Malay Mail, The Sun Daily, The Star etc. In 2020, He was named as Indonesia's Rising Star Lawyers by Asian Legal Business Magazine.

Hendra speaks Indonesian and English.

Alfin Frans Nainggolan

Alfin is a co-founder, principal and the Head of Corporate and Commercial Practice Group of F&S Law Office. He gained his lawyering experience through working for two major law firms in Indonesia. Prior to that, Alfin worked as an in house legal counsel in the largest real estate company and a prominent bank in Indonesia.

Alfin is highly valued by clients for his effective legal advice and knowledge on business matters. He was once appointed as a President Director of a subsidiary company (a construction company) within a large state-owned company group in Indonesia. During his term of office in the subsidiary company, he managed to restructure and raise the subsidiary company from collapsed and increase the margin of the subsidiary company from one digit to double digits.

Alfin has assisted numerous clients, both local and multinational, on various commercial transactions and formation of business entities in Indonesia. The businesses of the clients that he has assisted cover manufacturing, trading, mining, power and energy, advertising, tourism, construction, and property and real estate. On construction, property and real estate matters, Alfin is regarded as one of the leading lawyers.

Alfin is also known for his capability in assisting clients on anti-dumping matter. His broad connectivity to various business environments in Indonesia allows him to get access to necessary information and data for the purpose anti-dumping investigations.

Alfin obtained his Bachelor of Law degree from the University of Padjadjaran, Bandung – Indonesia in 2008. He is a member of the Indonesian Bar (PERADI). He also holds Certificate of Basic Management of Pension Fund in Indonesia.

Alfin speaks Indonesian, Russian and English.

Ricky Hasiholan

Prior joining F&S, Mr. Ricky worked for several years in some major law firms in Indonesia and as a Legal Manager in an Indonesian legal services company. Previously, he worked as a legal officer of Special Asset Management, Remedial and Litigation Division in a well-known bank in Indonesia.

During his career as a lawyer, Mr. Ricky has been involved in corporate, commercial, banking, property and capital markets projects. Mr. Ricky also has extensive experience in handling commercial litigation and alternative dispute resolution matters.

At F&S, Ricky mostly handles general corporate, corporate secretarial, direct investments, joint ventures, mergers & acquisitions, commercial litigation, company setup, and startup establishments projects, in the sector of energy & natural resources, IT, e-commerce, property and finance.

Ricky graduated from Faculty of Law of the University of Trisakti. He is a member of the Indonesian Bar (PERADI) and registered as Capital Market Legal Consultant (HKHPM) with the Indonesian Financial Services Authority (OJK).

Ricky speaks Indonesian and English.

Anindia Kusuma

Anindia is a Partner of F&S' Corporate and Commercial Practice Group. Before joining F&S, Anindia was the co-founder and managing partner of a boutique corporate law firm based in Kuningan, Jakarta. Prior to that, she worked for more than 7 years as a senior associate in some major Indonesian-based corporate law firms.

Anindia mostly handles non-litigation major corporate projects, such as direct investments, joint venture, initial public offerings, mergers & acquisitions, rights issues, company setup and other capital market and corporate financial matters, in the sectors of mining, plantation, property, shipping, health care, manufacturing & distribution and finance.

Anindia obtained her Bachelor of Law degree from University of Udayana, Indonesia. She also holds Master of Commercial Law degree from Deakin University, Australia.

Anindia is a member of the Indonesian Bar. She speaks Indonesian and English.
