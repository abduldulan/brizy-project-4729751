Dispute Resolutions Practice

In a conflicting situation, F&S lawyers help their clients to creatively and innovatively choose and execute the accurate strategy to manage or resolve the conflict at hand, either through litigation, arbitration, or other alternative dispute resolution (ADR) methods, in order to achieve success and peace of mind. F&S’s commitment is always to help its clients resolve the conflict creatively and efficiently, not merely to manage the process.

Over the years, F&S lawyers have been involved in many high profile litigation cases, both purely domestic and of cross-border nature. They have also experienced defending clients in bankruptcy proceedings before the Commercial Court as well as representing clients in claiming their rights from bankruptcy estates of companies declared bankrupt by the Commercial Court.

F&S’ Dispute Resolution practice is heavily engaged in all types of civil and commercial litigation, encompassing areas such as banking, finance, insurance, capital market, investment, acquisition, shareholders’ dispute and several other areas. It is noteworthy that F&S’ Managing Partner, Hendra Setiawan Boen, has been involved in a number of disputes before the State-Administration Court (PTUN) in cases where the interests of his clients were detrimentally affected by other parties.

Members of F&S’ Dispute Resolution practice have also honed their skills in business-competition dispute resolution through their involvement in a number of litigation cases before the Indonesian Business Competition Authority (KPPU), particularly through their involvement in two of the Authority’s most landmark and biggest cases—both of which were of cross-border nature.

In addition, out of their commitment and passion to uphold law and justice, members of F&S’ Dispute Resolution practice have also been involved in cases in which they defended human rights, freedom of the press, freedom of speech and freedom of expression.

Corporate and Commercial Practice

Led by Mr. Alfin Frans Nainggolan, F&S’ Corporate and Commercial practice advices and assists clients on various corporate and commercial legal matters such as company or legal entity formation, corporate compliance, merger and acquisition, company restructuring, liquidation, joint venture, joint operation, employment, financing, construction, and property and real estate.

F&S’ lawyers have successfully handled numerous domestic and cross-borders merger and acquisition transactions, and formation as well as liquidation of companies in various sectors of business such as manufacturing, trading, mining, power and energy, advertising, tourism, construction, and property and real estate.

F&S also assists both domestic and foreign clients on trade and anti-dumping matters. In assisting clients on these matters, F&S lawyers are experienced in conducting research and investigation both on regulatory and practical/factual aspects.

Intellectual Property Practice

In today’s highly competitive business environment, protection against imitation is the key to securing the benefits of innovation. F&S’ Intellectual Property practice assists clients in all aspects of protection and exploitation of new ideas. It advises on a wide range of intellectual property matters for diverse industries and provides services for all types of Intellectual Property work, ranging from contentious to non-contentious Intellectual Property legal services - including trademark, industrial design, copyright and patent. It also renders legal services concerning Intellectual-Property-related transactions to international and domestic clients. Industries that F&S’ Intellectual Property practice serves include food and beverages, chemical substances and materials, engineering services and products and materials, computers, apparels, pharmaceuticals, entertainment and several other industries.
