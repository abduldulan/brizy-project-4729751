Notes from the Founders

Welcome to Frans & Setiawan Law Office ("F&S")

F&S is founded in July 2019 as a result of a merger between Setiawan & Partners and Frans & Associates. F&S is lead by two driven and motivated young lawyers—Hendra Setiawan Boen, and Alfin Frans Nainggolan—who have accumulated considerable experience over the years in the Indonesian legal market.

Prior to coming together to found F&S, we, the two founding partners had known each other for quite some time, and since then we admire each other’s strong professionalism and dedication to the profession, an admiration which grows with the passage of time and one based on respect for the exceptional quality and specialization in law we see in each other.

F&S is designed to provide effective legal solutions for its clients. With the combined resources of the two merging firms, we believe that F&S will be able to provide full legal service to its clients.

At F&S, lawyering is more than just a profession, it is a passion.

Values and Commitments

We maintain the high standard of ethical conducts. We seriously value client’s trust and confidence in us. Our commitment is not to abuse client’s trust and confidence. Our client’s interest will always be placed above our own, and drawing on their extensive experience serving high profile clients, F&S’ personnel have steadfast commitment to provide premium legal services. Notwithstanding the premium nature of its legal services, F&S is always committed to tailoring a reasonable and efficient fee structure for its clients.

F&S is fully committed to avoiding unnecessary prolongation of a case and exorbitant cost on the part of the client when certain issues can be effectively and efficiently resolved through reasonable negotiation. F&S is also fully committed to advising its clients in the clients’ best interest, including concerning costs incurred.

It merits emphasizing that all F&S’ personnel steadfastly adhere to the ethical standard of the legal profession.

Dispute Resolutions Practice
